Our Journey

A single drive to a lasting legacy

2003-2004

Laying the Foundation

The JP Group story began on the road. In 2003, with a ₹2000 salary and endless determination, the founder started as a driver planting the seeds of a dream built on honest hard work.

2004–2008

First Step into Aggregates Supply

That dream took shape in 2004 with a single tractor supplying stone aggregates. By 2008, the first truck joined the journey, turning effort into enterprise.

2011–2016

Establishing JP Blue Metals

As experience grew, focus sharpened. Transitioning from sand to Blue Metal, JP Blue Metals was born, laying crusher foundations in Kuppam and expanding fleets across Andhra Pradesh.

2020–2024

Multi-Location Crusher Expansion

With trust earned and demand rising, JP Group expanded its footprint. New crusher plants in Gudiyatham and Hosur marked a decisive step into large-scale operations.

2026-Present

Strengthening the Legacy

Today, with the launch of Ramana Stone Crusher in Hosur, JP Group moves forward carrying forward a legacy shaped by resilience, growth, and a commitment to building stronger foundations for the future.
