Engineered Stone & Aggregate Products for Stronger Infrastructure

M-Sand (Manufactured Sand)

Crushed sand manufactured to construction standards, ideal for concrete works and structural applications requiring uniform strength.

P-Sand (Plastering Sand)

Finely graded sand processed for plastering applications, ensuring smooth finishes and consistent surface quality.

20 MM Crushed Stone Aggregates
